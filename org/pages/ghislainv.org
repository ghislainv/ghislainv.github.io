*** Background

*November 2005 -- February 2009:* Cemagref de Grenoble (now [[https://www.lessem.fr/dynamics/][INRAE]]) --
[[https://www.agroparistech.fr/][AgroParisTech]]. /PhD/. Structuring uncertainty and variability

*2003 -- 2004:* [[https://www.mnhn.fr/][National Museum of Natural History (MNHN)]] -- [[https://www.agroparistech.fr/][INA-PG]] --
